FAQs


Why should a customer choose Homestays over Hotel accommodation?

Homestays are a better form of travel accommodation as they have the following advantages:

Location - Generally in a prime / premium location of the city

Rate - Reasonable rates as compared to hotel rates

Quality of room - Neat, clean with all amenities of a hotel room

Homely Food - Quality and hygienic home food customized to individual taste

Personalised service - Can enjoy personalised interactions and attention


How do I Book a Homestay?

Search for the City you would like to visit. You will find price and booking options against available homestays of the city.


Do I need to confirm the booking again?

Your booking stands confirmed once you get the payment receipt and confirmation voucher from the host. You can call our customer care for any assistance 24x7.


How can I modify my Booking?

To amend a Homestay booking, please contact our call center on 0120-6907420 (all networks).


Will I be charged if I wish to cancel my Homestay booking?

Yatra does not charge any Yatra fee for Homestay cancellation. However, the concerned Homestay Host might charge a fee for cancellation. You can either check on your confirmation voucher or call our call center for details.


How can I cancel my Booking?

You can cancel your booking by logging into your account or calling our customer care numbers. Yatra does not charge on Homestay cancellation. However cancellation charges would apply as per individual homestay cancellation policy.
